Subject: What if we could modernise post?..

From: josh@mailtrailing.com

Hey there,

I had this idea the other night that you might be interested in.

So a little background on the problem - you aren't always living the same place that your post expects you to be. You might be living in another city or another country from where your last address was. You might be at University or have moved house in, well, forever.

You should still be able to get hold of the post that’s addressed to you and not have to wait for your parents or friends to pass it on, if they remember!

What if you could have one personalised, secure address to send your post to, then have all the post that goes to that address accessible online? - as easy as you access your emails, whenever, wherever.

Sound good?
Let me know if you’re interested!
