Mail Trail? What's that then?

The idea here is fairly simple. You aren't always living the same place that your post expects you to be. You might be living in another city or another country from where your last address was. You might be at University or have moved house in the last, well forever.

Anyway, the point is you still get post that's address to you, even if your not there. This post is like email, some is important, some is crap. Either way, you want to know what it is so that you can respond to it, if you need to.

Ever had your parents scan you a letter that just came through? Or have them forget about that letter that came the other week?

What can you do about it?

Well right now, not so much. But we might be able to help you out.

Our system, once ready would be a hack on top of our beloved aging postal service. Once we're up and running we give you a personal address to which all your post can be sent. Then anything that is sent to this physical address will be picked up by one of our team, scanned in and sent to you by email. We'll even respond by post if that's required by the sender - except in legal cases (we're not really ready to figure that challenge out just yet!).

If you're interested please sign up with this link to a typeform. We'll keep your details safe, then get back to you as soon as we've got something working together.

Thanks for your time! The Mail Trail Team
